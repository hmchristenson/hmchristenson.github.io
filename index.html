I am a PhD Candidate and National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellow at UC Riverside working with Prof. George Becker. My research interests include cosmic reonization, high-redshift galaxies, and the intergalactic medium. I am currently studying the relationship between galaxy density and the opacity of the intergalactic medium, which can tell us about how and when reionization ended.

When not doing science, I enjoy knitting, baking, tabletop RPGs, and hanging out with my cat!
